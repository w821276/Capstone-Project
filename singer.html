I Sing

Music is Theraputic!

The Beginning

As a child, my late father use to wake up the whole neighborhood by banging on pots and pans. He later grew up to be the musical director of several local choirs.

The Inherittance

I remember one of my first Big Gigs; It was a school musical play. I had a big solo in front of a crowd full when I was in kindergarten. My dress was so pretty and I had a voice to match it. I have not put down the microphone since then.

The Legacy

My children are ages 11, 6 and 2 . All three of them have vividly seen me behind the microphone while in the studio and stages. We love to write songs, play musical instruments and sing together. I just want to plant the seed. Time will show how the seed will grow.
